Common reasons leaders reach out

Frequent requests and states among leaders and entrepreneurs. Concrete tasks addressed in a confidential format.

  • Chronic anxiety & hyperarousal — tension, irritability, mental “grinding,” decision paralysis.
  • Burnout — exhaustion + cynicism + lower effectiveness; “I’m pulling, but not living.”
  • Sleep disruption — insomnia, early awakening, jet lag/late calls → attention lapses.
  • High-functioning depression — you perform, but "nothing brings joy"; drive and motivation drop.
  • Perfectionism & impostor syndrome — procrastination, micromanagement, fear of delegating.
  • Attention fragmentation / decision fatigue — endless notifications, context switching, no “deep work.”
  • Addictive patterns — caffeine/nicotine/alcohol “to take the edge off,” sleeping pills/benzos; sometimes stimulants.
  • Somatic stress — headaches/neck-back, IBS, skin, palpitations.
  • Leaders’ isolation, relationship strain — loneliness, conflicts at home, lower closeness/libido.
  • Existential emptiness — “I achieved a lot — what’s next?”
  • Ethical stress & guilt — layoffs, reputation risks → intrusive ruminations.
  • PTSD-like reactions to business traumas — hypervigilance, outbursts, avoidance.
  • Neurodiversity — ADHD/autistic spectrum: strengths × friction with routine/communication.
  • Metabolic risks — weight gain, high blood pressure, baseline inflammation (stress + flights + chaotic diet).
  • Over-control and responsibility — “it’s all on me,” fear of letting go → bottlenecks in the org system.
